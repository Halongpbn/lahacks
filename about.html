About us

- OUR GOAL -

To help people find places to park their bikes.

A B O U T

At Cyclr, we worked to develop a website that could help bicyclist find easier parking spots. Throughout everyday situations, we have constantly seen people using their bicycles as transportation to go to work or simply exercise. We have seen people struggle to find nearby biking racks in order to park. Thus, we have came up with the idea of this website.

We would also like to promote the transportation of biking. We are all members of the Green Team at Carlmont High School. This topic is very important to us and we would like to spread the enviornmental ideas to all. Everyday, the majority of people uses cars to go to their destinations. This impacts our enviornment greatly through the amount of carbon dioxide intoxicating the air. Although this amount may not be as much compared to factories, we would like to lessen the amount of carbon emissions in our atmosphere. With this in mind, we have developed this website in order to encourge everyone to bike more often.
